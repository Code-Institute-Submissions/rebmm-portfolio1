Who is Pawkies?

Pawkies is a service provided for walking our friends with paws
We LOVE dogs and want to help those who may not have the time or struggle walking their dogs
We also provide training sessions to owners who struggle controlling their dog on the leash
We host events for all owners and pets to come together to improve dog socialisation, and humans!
Regulars and newcomers always welcome with open arms, and paws!
